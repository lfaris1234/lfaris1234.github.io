2022-2025

After graduating into a pretty rough job market, I moved to San Diego! My plan was to live cheaply while working on personal projects and making money anywhere I can until I found a full-time role that excited me. I've only been here for about a week but I've learned how to build a fence, remulch a yard, replace my breakpads and rotors, and enjoy a burrito after a hard day's work like I never have before. No matter the stress, I'm a pretty lucky guy.
