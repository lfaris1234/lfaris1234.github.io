Workaway in Greece: tech fixes by the sea

August 16, 2025

This summer (2025) I traveled to Greece for about a month and spent a few weeks in Porto Rafti on a Workaway. I helped my work host— a postdoc researcher in wrongful convictions and criminology (and an author) — untangle some seriously messed-up website backend issues. Along the way I learned a new hosting platform, Host Europe, and picked up a lot of hands-on web dev and data maintenance skills. It was a really fun reminder that I can keep learning in tech everywhere I go.
